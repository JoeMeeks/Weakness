FFXI PROC

FFXI Weakness Triggers (PROC)

Notorious Monsters in Abyssea and Voidwatch and all in Dynamis have vulnerabilites that are procedural random occurances, or “PROC”s.

Abyssea Notorious Monsters

  • Weapon Skills:
    Atmas and NM key items
  • Magic Spells:
    EX Empyrean armor upgrade items
  • Weapon Skills:
    RA/EX weapons and armor

Dynamis (# of Time Extensions)

  • 3× TE:
    single currency drop slot
  • 4× TE:
    additional currency drop slot
  • 5× TE:
    additional 100% drop slot
  • No sub job (CoP areas):
    100% hundred-piece drop slot
